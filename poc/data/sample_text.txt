Elon Musk founded SpaceX in 2002 with the goal of revolutionizing space technology. SpaceX is headquartered in Hawthorne, California. Elon Musk serves as the CEO of SpaceX, which developed the Falcon 9 rocket and Dragon spacecraft.

Tesla, where Elon Musk serves as CEO, is headquartered in Austin, Texas. Tesla manufactures the Model 3 and Model Y electric vehicles. In a major business move, Elon Musk acquired Twitter (now X) in 2022 for $44 billion, and now serves as its CEO.

In the AI field, Sam Altman and Elon Musk co-founded OpenAI in 2015. Sam Altman led OpenAI as CEO until 2023, during which OpenAI developed ChatGPT and released it in November 2022. Microsoft has formed a strategic partnership with OpenAI, investing billions in the company.

The tech industry competition continues to evolve. Google, under CEO Sundar Pichai, developed Bard as a direct competitor to ChatGPT. Meanwhile, Meta, led by CEO Mark Zuckerberg, has invested heavily in virtual reality technology, focusing on developing the metaverse platform.
